Questions?

About 3-4 hours per week. The modules are short (5-10 min videos), and you can follow at your own pace. Live sessions are 1 hour weekly.

We work with the best available AI tools — including ChatGPT, Claude, automation platforms, and agent frameworks. Everything is explained step by step.

Yes. If you start self-paced and want to upgrade to the live bootcamp, you only pay the difference.
